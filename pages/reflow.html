Adobe Reflow

As responsive websites have become the standard in web design and development companies are trying to make tools that simplify the process of creating them. Applications like Macaw and Adobe Reflow to help the process. There are a host of others from DivShot to JetStrap and more like them. Back when Adobe released Reflow I went ahead and created a mock layout to see how the program worked and to discover if there were ways to make the design and development phase more efficient.
